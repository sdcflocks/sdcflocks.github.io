Welcome to the Slambooke Help Center

---------------------------------------------



What is Slambooke anyway?

Slambooke is an information network made up of pages about Schools. It's an easy way to discover the latest news related to schools in Ghana you care about.

How is it useful?

Slambooke contains information you will find valuable. It’s like being delivered a newspaper whose headlines you’ll always find interesting – you can discover news as it’s happening, learn more about schools that are important to you, and get the inside scoop in real time.

How to start using Slambooke?

If you are viewing this page, then you already know your way through Slambooke. Some people find it useful to contribute their taughts, but the real magic of Slambooke lies in absorbing real-time information that matters to you other than contributing yourself.

Where can i use Slambooke?

Take It With You: Connect with your pc, smartphone and tablet: One of the best things about Slambooke is that it’s portable. Now you can get updates about any schools in Ghana in the middle of your commute or catch up on the buzz about an event you’re about to attend.
