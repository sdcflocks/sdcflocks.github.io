Greenhill

Greenhill University College


The Greenhill College which is part of GIMPA as beem set up to encourage full-time employees to acquir Bachelors Degrees in in three and four years in the case of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) degre, without having to resign from their employements. The programme runs in the afternoons to evenings from 4:00pm - 8:00pm.


Candidates must be 25years and above and must have work for at least 3years (except ICT candidates). ICT candidates may be admitted with their SHS qualifications without age and work experience limitations. The ICT programme is a four year programme. The College runs two semesters a year and the first programme is from August to December and the second runs from February to June.

Some of the College's strength include distiguished and dedicated faculty, personalised attention to students, small class size per programme, morden first-class classrooms with comfortable sitting fo each student, low faculty/student ratio, high legal, moral and ethical standards, electronic library and access to free internet by registered students, accreditation by the national accreditation board.
