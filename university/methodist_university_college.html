MUCG

Methodist University College Ghana


The role of the Methodist church in providing basic secondary and teacher education has further been broadened with the introduction of the Methodist University. The plans and decisions were approved at the Methodist Church's 37th Annual Conference held in Sunyani in 1998.


The mission of the college is to promote and develop academic excellence, spirituality, morality and service within the Ghanaian society. This will involve the attainment of the total development of the individuals who pass through the Methodist University College,Ghana (MUCG) mentally, spiritually and physically. On the basis of Christian principles, the institution will blend academic, religious and profesional excellenc leading to an appreciation of globalization,scientific and technological as well as cultural changes and innovations.


The Univristy College is accredited by the National Accreditation Board to offer first-degree programmes in usiness Administration and Social Studies and it is affiliated to the University of Ghana. The University College has three campuses: The initial campus of the University Collegeis at the southern portion of the WesleyGrammar School grounds, Dansoman, Accra and the others are located in Tema and Wenchi in the Brong Ahafo Region. The Tema Campus runs only usiness Administraton programmes which are currently availale in the evenings only.


However, it is envisaged that the University will ultimately be a multi-campus institution. The campuses will house different faculties to be located in Accra, Bonwire, Dawhenya, Ejura, Koforidual, Kumasi, Larteh, Techiman and Wenchi.


Admission could be obtained for a 4,3, or 2year Day time or Evening time programmes, depending on qualifications and conveniences of candidates. There are two semesters. The first is from Septemer/Octoer to February, while the secondruns from March to July.
